# MarketHub Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP Foundation, Months 1-6

Goal: Launch a usable multi-vendor marketplace for customers, vendors, and admins.

Core work:

- Customer-facing storefront with home, categories, stores, deals, product listing, wishlist, cart, checkout, and order tracking.
- Vendor registration, onboarding, storefront profile, product upload, inventory management, order processing, and basic analytics.
- Admin dashboard for vendor approval, category management, product moderation, order monitoring, commissions, payouts, and dispute review.
- Mobile Money payment support through providers such as MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Telecel Cash, and M-Pesa.
- Email, SMS, push, and WhatsApp notification foundation.
- Reviews and ratings for products and vendors.

Success criteria:

- Customers can complete a full purchase flow.
- Vendors can create products and process orders.
- Admins can approve vendors, monitor orders, and manage marketplace operations.
- Payments and notifications are reliable enough for production pilot users.

## Phase 2: Growth Features, Months 7-12

Goal: Improve retention, monetization, and marketplace intelligence.

Core work:

- AI product recommendations.
- Loyalty program.
- Affiliate marketing.
- Premium vendor subscriptions.
- Sponsored product placements.
- Improved vendor analytics.
- Customer personalization based on browsing, search, and purchase history.

Success criteria:

- Increased repeat purchase rate.
- Vendors can pay for visibility and premium features.
- Customers receive useful recommendations and promotions.

## Phase 3: Scale and B2B, Year 2

Goal: Expand MarketHub from retail marketplace into wholesale and regional commerce.

Core work:

- Wholesale product flows.
- B2B marketplace features.
- Logistics partner network.
- Regional expansion tools.
- Bulk ordering.
- Negotiated pricing.
- Advanced inventory and fulfillment workflows.

Success criteria:

- Wholesalers and business buyers can transact at scale.
- Logistics integrations support multiple cities and regions.
- Platform can support larger catalogs and higher order volume.

## Phase 4: Advanced Commerce, Year 3+

Goal: Build competitive advantages that make MarketHub a regional commerce ecosystem.

Core work:

- Live commerce.
- Auction marketplace.
- Cross-border trade.
- Embedded financing.
- AI vendor assistant.
- AI fraud detection.
- AI customer assistant.

Success criteria:

- MarketHub supports new transaction models beyond standard retail.
- Vendors can access financing and automation tools.
- Customers can shop through intelligent assistant experiences.
