## App Name

MarketHub

## Short Description

Buy and sell products across Ghana with trusted sellers.

## Full Description

MarketHub is a Ghana-focused multi-vendor marketplace where customers can discover products, compare sellers, shop retail items, request wholesale deals and connect with approved sellers.

MarketHub helps shoppers browse categories, find local sellers and explore product photos and videos. Approved sellers can create product listings, manage stock and publish products for buyers across Ghana.

Payments are currently being prepared and must be clearly labelled as test mode until secure live payment integration is completed.

## Key Features

- Shop products from approved sellers
- Browse categories and deals
- Sellers can upload products with pictures and videos
- Mobile-friendly shopping experience
- Buyer and seller messaging
- Product reporting and moderation
- Seller dashboard
- Ghana-focused marketplace experience
