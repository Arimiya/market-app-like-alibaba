## App Name

MarketHub

## Short Description

Buy and sell products across Ghana with trusted sellers.

## Full Description

MarketHub is a Ghana-focused multi-vendor marketplace where customers can discover products, compare sellers, shop retail items, request wholesale deals and connect with approved sellers.

MarketHub helps buyers browse categories, view product pictures and videos, save products, message sellers and shop from local vendors. Approved sellers can create product listings, manage stock and publish products immediately, while MarketHub provides reporting and moderation tools to keep the marketplace safer.

Online payments are currently being prepared and checkout must remain clearly labelled as test mode until secure live payment verification is completed.

## Key Features

- Browse products and categories
- Shop from approved sellers
- Sellers can upload products with pictures and videos
- Product reporting and moderation
- Buyer and seller messaging
- Mobile-friendly shopping experience
- Ghana-focused marketplace
