# AI Features

## Purpose

AI features are a future competitive advantage for MarketHub, but they should be introduced after the MVP data foundation is stable.

## AI Product Recommendations

- Personalized product suggestions.
- Frequently bought together.
- Similar products.
- Deal recommendations.

## AI Fraud Detection

- Suspicious transactions.
- Fake reviews.
- Unusual account behavior.
- Risk scoring for orders and vendors.

## AI Vendor Assistant

- Product description generation.
- Sales forecasting.
- Inventory forecasting.
- Promotion recommendations.

## AI Customer Assistant

- Shopping chatbot.
- Order tracking assistant.
- Product comparison assistant.
- Refund and support guidance.

## Implementation Note

AI should depend on reliable marketplace data, clear privacy rules, and human review for high-risk decisions such as fraud, refunds, and vendor suspension.
