# Product Management

## Purpose

Product management allows vendors to create, update, organize, and track products across the marketplace.

## Product Fields

- Product name.
- SKU.
- Description.
- Images.
- Videos.
- Brand.
- Category.
- Price.
- Discount price.
- Stock quantity.
- Shipping weight.

## Product Types

- Physical products.
- Digital products.
- Services.
- Wholesale products.

## Inventory Requirements

- Stock tracking.
- Low stock alerts.
- Bulk updates.
- CSV import and export.
- Product status controls: draft, active, out of stock, hidden, suspended.

## Admin Requirements

- Moderate product content.
- Suspend products that violate marketplace policy.
- Manage categories and attributes.
- Review suspicious listings.
