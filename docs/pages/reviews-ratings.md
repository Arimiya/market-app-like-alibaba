# Reviews and Ratings

## Purpose

Reviews and ratings help customers trust products and vendors while giving admins tools to moderate marketplace quality.

## Product Reviews

- Star rating.
- Written review.
- Photos.
- Videos.
- Verified purchase badge.

## Vendor Reviews

- Store rating.
- Delivery rating.
- Customer service rating.

## Core Requirements

- Allow reviews only for completed or delivered orders.
- Mark reviews as verified when tied to a real purchase.
- Support moderation for abuse, spam, and fake reviews.
- Calculate product and vendor rating averages.

## MVP Data Needed

- Review author.
- Product.
- Vendor.
- Order.
- Rating.
- Review text.
- Media attachments.
- Moderation status.
