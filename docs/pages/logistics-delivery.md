# Logistics and Delivery

## Purpose

Logistics and delivery connect MarketHub orders to courier, postal, pickup, and third-party fulfillment workflows.

## Delivery Partners

- Local courier services.
- National postal services.
- Third-party logistics providers.

## Core Features

- Real-time tracking.
- Delivery estimation.
- Delivery fee calculator.
- Pickup points.
- Vendor pickup scheduling.
- Shipment status updates.

## Order Statuses

- Pending.
- Paid.
- Processing.
- Packed.
- Shipped.
- Delivered.
- Returned.
- Refunded.
- Cancelled.

## MVP Requirements

- Store delivery address per order.
- Assign delivery method.
- Store tracking number when available.
- Notify customers when shipment status changes.
- Allow vendors to update fulfillment status.
